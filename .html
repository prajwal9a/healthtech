About Our SMS Alert System

Universal Accessibility

Works on all mobile phones, from basic feature phones to smartphones, without requiring internet connectivity.

Immediate Delivery

Critical health information reaches recipients within seconds, enabling immediate action when needed.

Two-Way Communication

Community members can respond to alerts, report symptoms, or ask questions directly via SMS.

Multi-Language Support

Alerts delivered in local languages to ensure understanding across diverse communities.

English Hindi Spanish French Arabic Swahili Bengali Portuguese
Geographic Targeting

Alerts can be sent to specific villages or regions, preventing unnecessary panic in unaffected areas.

Types of Health Alerts

Outbreak Alerts

Immediate notifications when disease outbreaks are detected in your area with specific prevention guidelines.

"Cholera alert in Village East. Boil all water. Avoid well #3. Health camp at community center tomorrow."

Water Safety Alerts

Notifications about contaminated water sources and alternative safe water options.

"Water test failed at well #2. Use well #5 or boiling method. New water purification tablets available at health center."

Vaccination Reminders

Schedule reminders for vaccination campaigns and individual vaccination appointments.

"Measles vaccination tomorrow at school ground, 9am-3pm. Bring vaccination card. Children 6 months to 15 years."

Prevention Tips

Regular health education messages about hygiene, nutrition, and disease prevention.

"Health Tip: Wash hands with soap before eating and after toilet use. This prevents diarrhea and other diseases."
